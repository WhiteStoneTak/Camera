What is a Data Scientist?

A data scientist is a professional who extracts valuable insights from large amounts of data. They combine statistical knowledge, programming, and business knowledge to support data-driven decision-making.

Required Skills

  • Programming languages (Python, R)
  • Statistics and machine learning
  • Database (SQL)
  • Data visualization
  • Business analysis
