My Milestones

2007

My birthplace is in Japan; according to my mother, the day I was born was a beautiful day, and there were birds singing.

2010

This year was when I started skiing. At that time, I was too afraid to ski, and my father would take me to the bottom of the ski slope.

2011

My parents put me in an English class. I was so upset, but met my best friend there.

2017

My father, who has an experience with cello, had a huge influence on me; I started playing cello.

2022

I participated to a speech contest. It was a difficult experience, but also fun. The speech lasted for 3 minutes, and I was awarded 3rd place in that contest.

2023

In this year, I arrived to Canada to study. The diversity among people had me notice the attractiveness of Canada. Since my hobby is to ski, I had skied many times last winter.

2024

My love for staying outside led me to go to most parts of Vancouver. I spent a lot of time with my friends outside. My English improved overtime, without realizing it.

2025

Now that my English has improved, interacting with people has been more enjoyable. What surprised me the most is that, I could keep a conversation with my cousin in English. My goal for the future is to improve more intellectually, and work with people from other countries.
